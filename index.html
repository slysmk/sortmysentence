What is SortMySentence?

SortMySentence is an interactive learning platform designed to help you understand English grammar naturally. Instead of memorizing rules, you build sentences by arranging words in the correct order.

Why This Method Works

  • Improves sentence structure intuition
  • Strengthens vocabulary in context
  • Helps beginner (A1–B2) learners
  • Gamified learning increases retention

Practice Examples

Scrambled: “to / school / goes / she”

Correct: “She goes to school”


Scrambled: “playing / they / football / are”

Correct: “They are playing football”
